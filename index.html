Research Kitchens

The website is dedicated to Research Kitchens. The NYUAD Research Kitchens are thematic hubs for building communities of researchers across disciplinary boundaries. The Kitchens are an initiative of the Arts and Humanities division at NYU Abu Dhabi.

seks.design

seks.design is a collective that explores radical solutions for community resilience through the “Y-curiosity complex”. By embracing local-first, future-forward approaches that blend child-like curiosity with intentional uses of technology. We host spaces that focus on collective storytelling, urban ritual design, and play.

Glamorous Edge

Glamorous Edge is a ladies' salon offering a comprehensive range of beauty and wellness services. The salon specializes in hair styling, makeup, nail treatments, and more. With a focus on elegance and personalized care, Glamorous Edge caters exclusively to women and girls seeking high-quality beauty experiences in a comfortable and stylish setting.

YBerlin

TYBerlin is a collective ecosystem in Berlin next to Treptower Park that hosts regenerative residencies and experience design offerings. Often dubbed as a “village in the city”, YBerlin has been an ecosystem provider for diverse communities focused on building connection in Berlin.
