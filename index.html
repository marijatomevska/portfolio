Projects

30MFF Website

The website is dedicated to showcasing the cats at NYUAD. It features a gallery of the feline residents, monthly cat counts, and provides a way to contact the project organizers. The project's overall concept and theme aim to create an informative and engaging experience for visitors interested in the campus's cats.

Comix Strip

“Overtime” is an interactive comic that narrates the story of an employee who doesn’t want to go to work. Throughout the story, users are presented with various choices, potentially leading to job loss, or the more drastic option of confronting the boss, resulting in jail-time.

Sound Project

"Tourist" is an immersive sound project designed to encapsulate the essence of arriving in a new city. Our vision was to create an experience that mirrors the journey of a tourist from the moment they land at the airport to their arrival at the hotel.

Video Project

The concept of "Survival Guide" is a practical, yet engaging Survival Guide specifically tailored to handling flooding incidents on a university campus in the UAE. The theme merges humor with gravity, balancing light-hearted elements with essential survival tactics.
